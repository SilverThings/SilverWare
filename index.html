The Details

SilverWare.io Features

Experience the future of Microservices development

Easy to use

SilwerWare builds on top of existing technologies and frameworks you are familiar with (REST, CDI (Weld), Camel, Vert.x, NoSQL, ActiveMQ, Amazon SQS, Hystrix...).

Modularized

SilverWare is modularized, highy configurable and container-free. Your deployments only carry the minimum code to run independently. A developer picks up individual components that they need for their Microservices.

Reuse existing services

You can immediately deploy any of your existing services written in those frameworks with SilverWare. Or you can start developing new services in a language of your taste including Java, Jython, jRuby, Scala, Ceylon, Kolin, Clojure...

Start the future now!
