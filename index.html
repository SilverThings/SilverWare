The Details

SilverWare.io Features

Experience the future of Microservices development

Easy to use

SilverWare builds on top of existing technologies and frameworks you are familiar with (CDI (Weld), Camel, REST, ActiveMQ, Vert.x, NoSQL, Amazon SQS, Hystrix...). We do not reinvent the wheel. No generated stubs and yet easy to debug.

Modularized

SilverWare is modularized, highy configurable and container-free. Your deployments only carry the minimum code to run independently. A developer picks up individual components that they need for their Microservices and starts developing their business code immediately.

Reuse existing services

You can immediately deploy any of your existing services written in the supported frameworks with SilverWare. Or you can start developing new services in a language of your taste including Java, Jython, jRuby, Scala, Ceylon, Kotlin, Clojure...

Start the future now!
